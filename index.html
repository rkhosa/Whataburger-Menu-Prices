Breakfast Menu

Whataburger's breakfast menu is served from 11 PM to 11 AM daily, featuring popular items like:

  • Breakfast on a Bun
  • Taquitos
  • Pancake Platter
  • Breakfast Platter

Prices for breakfast items range from $2.50 to $5.50, offering a variety of options to start your day.

Salads

For those seeking lighter options, Whataburger offers fresh salads such as:

  • Garden Salad
  • Grilled Chicken Salad
  • Apple & Cranberry Chicken Salad

Salad prices range from $5.99 to $7.99.

Drinks & Shakes

Complement your meal with a selection from Whataburger's drink menu, which includes:

  • Soft Drinks
  • Freshly Brewed Iced Tea
  • Milkshakes (Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry)
  • Coffee

Drink prices range from $1.29 to $3.29, depending on size and selection.

Limited-Time Offers

Whataburger frequently introduces limited-time specials to keep the menu exciting. As of March 2025, some of these offerings include:

  • Bacon Blue Cheese Burger
  • Peppercorn Ranch Chicken Club
  • Grilled Cheese with Grilled Vegetables
  • Chicken & Pancake
  • Jalapeño Cheddar Biscuit

Prices for these specials vary; check with your local Whataburger for current availability and pricing.

Customization

One of Whataburger's standout features is the ability to customize your burger with over 36,000 possible combinations of toppings, sauces, and sides, ensuring your meal is made just the way you like it.
